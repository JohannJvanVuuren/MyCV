Short bio

My career started as a BSc student, majoring in chemistry and biochemistry at the University of the Witwatersrand. It was also there that I had my first scientific position as laboratory assistant in the organic chemistry department. Before finishing my studies I was offered a position in the leading Pathology Laboratory in South Africa and spent the next 22 years working in clinical pathology.


Computer programming has been a hobby of mine throughout the years, starting at University and I soon realised that my heart yearns to work in the web and software development industry. After saving up enough life savings, I finally decided to take the plunge and resigned from my pathology career to pursue my dreams full time.
